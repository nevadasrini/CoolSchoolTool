About

Due to COVID-19, many students will be forced to experience this school year online. With clasSpace, students will be able to document what assignments they have, their due date, and will be able to join a chat room with their teacher all on one site!

How To Use

It's easy. Create an account to track your rooms and assignments. In the assignments page, you can add new assignments and manage upcoming tasks. In the rooms page, you can add new rooms and automatically join video conferences with the click of a button. Everything you need on one site!
